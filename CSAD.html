Crowd sourced accident detection.

CSAD

Turns images and geolocation tags of accidents into valuable data which can then be used to save lives.

Background:

More than 1.25 people are killed each year in traffic accidents. Out of these many are caused due to poor infrastructure or its mismanagement of road infrastructure. We provide a Crowd Sourced GPS locations of accidents are used to create a heatmap of accidents which can then be used by the government and civil agencies identify these locations and fix the problems.
